Baybayin tools in one focused app.

Kudlit brings scanning, translation, lessons, glyph references, and companion guidance into a single mobile learning flow.

Start instantly

Sign in or continue as guest. Google, phone OTP, and email options — no account needed to explore.

Scan Baybayin glyphs

Camera and gallery-based recognition with clear fallback states and mobile-first scanner controls.

Translate both ways

Filipino to Baybayin and Baybayin to Filipino surfaces for everyday learning and practice.

Learn by doing

Lessons, glyph references, quiz paths, and sketch practice make Baybayin study feel hands-on.

Ask Butty

A friendly companion gives learning support, explanations, and guidance when symbols need context.

90-second demo path.

A short reviewer flow for trying Kudlit quickly from install to learning support.

  1. Install the Android APK from the v1.0.0 release.
  2. Continue as guest from the branded welcome screen.
  3. Open Scan and show the camera/gallery scanner controls.
  4. Switch to Translate and type a Filipino phrase.
  5. Open Learn to show lessons, glyph references, and quiz-oriented practice.
  6. Switch to Butty for companion explanations and learning guidance.
